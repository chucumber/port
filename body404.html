Body 404 [2025]
Body 404, an exhibition by ⤷ sangchi liu, imagines an “afterlife” space where AI and humans coexist. The work explores themes of identity, memory, and the fleeting nature of technology, reflecting a shared struggle to find meaning in what is temporary. As participants move through the space, they become part of its reflective landscape, observing their own presence within it.
