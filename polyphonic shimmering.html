Polyphonic Shimmering[2024]
⤷ visit the site

Polyphonic Shimmering is a publication authored, edited, and designed by participants in a seminar led by ⤷ Meg Miller during the spring of 2024. The seminar explored what would happen if writing were approached as a form of image-making.

The site is designed and coded with ⤷ Rasim Bayramov and ⤷ Weitong Sun.
