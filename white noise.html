White Noise[2019]
White Noise is the second production by ⤷ The Gluttons Assemblage, exploring life during Taiwan's White Terror, a period of political repression from 1947 to 1987.

The accompanying document folder contains statements, background stories, recommended readings, and character cards. Bound tightly with eyelets and presented in an intentionally disordered format, it invites the audience to tear through the cover and reconstruct fragmented stories. This interactive experience reflects the process of uncovering and rebuilding hidden histories.
