The Night We Jump into the Sea [2018]
The Night We Jump into the Sea tells the story of a group of young people who meet online and choose to end their lives together on a beach. The play addresses themes of social media and youth suicide.

The accompanying booklet includes tearing guidelines on each page, inviting the audience to engage and personalize it, echoing the play's central message of embracing differences.
