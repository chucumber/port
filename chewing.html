Chewing on a Flash Drive [2024]
⤷ Chewing on a Flas Drive reflects on memory and impermanence, sparked by a discarded flash drive I found on the street. That damaged drive becomes a vessel for contemplation about connection, migration, and lost data. Like digital storage, our memories can blur, rewrite themselves, or unexpectedly return. This work explores the quiet stories hidden in abandoned objects and the traces we leave behind as we journey through life.
