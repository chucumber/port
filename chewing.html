Chewing on a Flash Drive [2023]
⤷ Chewing on a Flas Drive started with a flash drive lying on the ground.

Despite its flash memory being unusable, the flash drive persisted as a storage medium. Its presence was a lasting reminder of the day they met it on the ground——the sparkling streets, cloudless sky, piled leaves, irresistible black hole, familiar eyes, their ongoing thoughts on loss and return——an abundance of data that took up zero bytes.
