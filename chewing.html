Chewing on a Flash Drive [2024]
⤷ Chewing on a Flas Drive started with a lost USB drive found on the street, sparking reflections on memory, loss, and the fleeting nature of both digital and human experiences. Like digital storage, our memories fade, rewrite, or unexpectedly return. This work pondered how ordinary objects——abandoned flash drives, discarded gum——carry traces of our past and connections, continuing the stories as their journey move on.
